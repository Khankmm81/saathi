Overview

Grocery • Medical • Education

Saathi is an common platform to provide below social services in professional way

 

  • Identify needy people.

  • Validate and verify them by doing BC.

  • Register them in Saathi platform.

  • Provide Grocery Help.

  • Provide Medical help.

  • Provide Education help.

  • Maintain Track Record.

Vision and Mission

High Level


Sponsor Enrollment

  • Identify the sponsor.
  • Setup a meeting request with sponsor.
  • Explain detail process of Saathi workflow.
  • Enroll sponsor with selected package.
  • Generate Saathi account for sponsor.
  • Receive/Get Charity from sponsor.
  • Allocate fund to Needy.
  • Share the monthly report with sponsors.

 

Enroll Needy Request

  • Identify Needy person.
  • Background check of Needy.
  • Categorize Needy.
  • Enrollment upon approval.
  • Allocate package based on category.
  • Package Delivery.
  • Maintain the track.

Our Team

Our sponsors, Approvers and Background Check [BC] is our team.

Work Flow

Call to action 1

  • Request via call / direct contact with Saathi team.
  • Background Check team will do back ground check and approve/reject the request.

Call to action 2

  • Approvers validate the approved request and map the request with category-package based on the budget.
  • Request goes to Execution team.

Call to action 3

  • Execution team will deliver/handover the package to door step/service provider and upload the receipt.
  • Audit team/sponsor can check with service availed person directly at anytime for audit.
